About Characters

All Characters in Honkai: Star Rail have two main defining attributes:

  1. their Element
  2. their Path

Characters Element

This attribute of theirs determine what type of Damage they deal, and what type of Breaks they can trigger if they deplete the toughness of an enemy.

These are the following available Elements:

  • Fire
  • Wind
  • Ice
  • Lightning
  • Physical
  • Quantum
  • Imaginary

It also limits what relics can be used on the character. As an example, a Fire character cannot benefit from a Quantum Damage increase.

Characters Path

This attribute works as a sort of 'class'. Each Path specialises in a different thing, making them distinct from each other. However we will see that sometimes even inside a certain Path, there can be characters that utilize the gimmick of the path quite differently.

These are the following available Paths:

Path Icon Path Name Path Description
Hunt Deals extraordinary amounts of single-target damage. The main damage dealer against Elite Enemies.
Erudition Deals remarkable amounts of multi-target damage. The main damage dealer against groups of enemies.
Destruction Deals outstanding amounts of damage and possesses great survivability. Suitable for various combat scenarios.
Abundance Heals allies, restores HP to the team, as well as offering debuff cleansing most of the time.
Preservation Possesses powerful defensive abilities to protect allies in various ways.
Harmony Applies buffs to allies to improve the team's combat capacities.
Nihility Applies debuffs to enemies to reduce their combat capacities.
